### QT-009: Danh mục dùng chung (P0)

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Status** | **FAIL** (automation) |
| **Severity** | Medium |

**Problem:** The sub-item "Danh mục dùng chung" resolved in DOM but click timed out. The sub-item is at position 14 of 20 in the expanded list, which may require scrolling in the sidebar. Playwright clicked the first sub-item ("Chương trình đào tạo") instead because `.first()` was used.

**Root cause:** Script used `.first()` which picked "Chương trình đào tạo" (item 1) instead of "Danh mục dùng chung" (item 14). Then timeout because Đào tạo module page took too long or navigation failed.

**Not a product bug** — automation selector issue. The sub-item exists in the DOM.

## Key Discovery: SLA Configuration Page

The SLA config page (`/quan-tri/cau-hinh-sla`) is the central control for time-based alerts across the entire system. What it confirms:

1. **4 entity types configured:** HOI_DAP (10 days), HOI_BLDT (10 days), HO_SO_DT (2 days), VU_VIEC (10 days)
2. **4-level color alerts:** Green → Yellow → Orange → Red bars (BR-SLA-02)
3. **Notification channels:** Both Email and App notifications are configurable per entity type
4. **Overdue tracking:** "Hồ sơ quá hạn" column tracks overdue count per type

This single page validates BR-SLA-01 through BR-SLA-04 across the whole system.
